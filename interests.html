My Interests

Flooofy

Over the past few years, I've slowly worked to open an online shop that sells craft patterns, mugs, shirts, hoodies and more. I started small, just selling crochet patterns and graphs.

This past Christmas was my busiest season. I also became the seller of a best-selling product; a Dolly Parton inpired mug, which you can see here.

I've currently made over 700 sales! Though I'm not as active as I was with designing new products, I do plan to continue growing my shop over time.

Yarn!

What can I say about yarn? It's awesome! It's addictive and it's relaxing.

I started playing with crochet when I was around 9 years old. My best friend taught me the basic chain stitch. From there, I began teaching myself. It took a few years before I was able to understand patterns that other people have written.

Eventually, I started writing my own patterns and I also started selling them. Over the last couple of years, I have also learned how to knit. It was pretty difficult to switch from one skill to the other, but thankfully I was able to combine the skills to make my projects look and feel even better.

Gaming

I've been playing computer games since I can remember. I remember playing on a Commadore 64, way back in the day! I eventually got into more role-playing games like Baldur's Gate, Myst, Riven and more.

Around 1999, I was introduced to my first MMORPG, EverQuest. I was hooked. I played a Woodelf druidess, Tyriess Mystwolfe. I also started playing with HTML around the same time and helped maintain a webpage for my server's guild list.

I've played far too many games to list here! My most-played game would have to be World of Warcraft. No matter what happens, or how many times I've quit (there have been more than a few) I keep coming back. I started WoW in 2005 and I'm currently leveling up for the next expansion.
